About Us

Pioneers in Digital Tile Innovation

Established in 2015, Stonex Ceramics has been at the forefront of the digital tile revolution in India. Based in Morbi, Gujarat – the ceramic hub of the country – we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge digital printing technology to create tiles that are not just products but works of art for your spaces.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans over 50,000 square feet, equipped with the latest European machinery that ensures precision, consistency, and exceptional quality in every tile we produce. From sourcing the finest raw materials to implementing rigorous quality control measures, we maintain the highest standards throughout our production process.
